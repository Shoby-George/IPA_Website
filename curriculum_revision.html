CURRICULUM REVISIONS

Based on the feedback collected from students, alumni, industries and employers faculty member may submit a proposed revision to the curriculum for which he or she has responsibility. After initial discussions with his or her colleagues regarding the proposed revision, the faculty member must submit the appropriate proposal. Upon submission of the proposed curriculum revision, the programme coordinator review and approve/reject on the proposed change. By approving/rejecting the proposal, the programme coordinator indicates that all processes were followed in accordance with curriculum approval policies. The approved curriculum revision is submitted to the Registrar to update any necessary systems.
