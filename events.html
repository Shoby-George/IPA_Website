2015
  • 2 Day Workshop on NI LabVIEW was conducted on 6th and 7th November2015 at NITC by Mr.Rajasekheran, Innovative Invaders Technologies (authorised academic reseller) National Instruments. The workshop mainly covered NI LabVIEW, NI Multisim Co-simulation for Power Electronics applications. Demonstration of NI myRIO, cRIO were also done.
  • 3 Day Advanced Workshop on "FPGA Control of Motor Drives" by Entuple Technologies was conducted on 4th, 5th and 6th November 2015. The workshop was based on the familiarization of the hardware interfacing modules used in real time FPGA control of motor drives. The control algorithm was demonstrated with FPGA control board and it was successfully implemented in the speed control of SRM and vector controlled induction motor drive.
  • ABB IRB1200 6DOF ROBOT The installation of the robot was carried out by Edutech on 30th October 2015. Theoretical and handson training sessions were given. The control of robot from RobotStudio, virtual flexpentent and real time flexpentent were demonstrated.
  • Workshop on "FPGA control of motor drives" by ENTUPLE technologies was conducted on 18th September 2015. The workshop was based on the familiarization of the hardware interfacing modules used in real time FPGA control of motor drives. The control algorithm was demonstrated with FPGA control board and it was successfully implemented in the speed control of BLDC Motor.
  • dSPACE Workshop was held on 22nd August 2015 which dealt with "Real time mechatronic control of motors for automotive applications" organized by Industrial Power group in association with DynaFusion Technologies. It was mainly focused in the demonstration of hardware devices involved in real time applications of dSPACE. Then real time PWM pulses were generated with the help of controller board with the help of dSPACE.
