Industrial Audit

As a part of the 1st semester course work in Energy Audit and Management (EE6401) students have to visit an industry, identify energy saving opertunities and conduct preliminary energy audit, suggest preliminary energy saving measures and identify scope for detailed energy audit.

Some of the industries where the students have visited are:

  • COMMONWEALTH TILE FACTORY, FEROKE
  • PRECOT MERIDIAN
  • Arrapetta Tea Factory
  • Odyssia footwear , Kozhikode
  • SUNLIT INDUSTRIES
  • P.P.S STEELS KANJI KODE, PALAKKAD
  • PEEKAY STEELS
  • Royal Cool Solutions Ltd
